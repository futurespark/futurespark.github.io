Day 1

Knowledge on current technologies, its emerging markets and ways to penetrate them are imperative to open up your mind towards future technologies and entrepreneurship. Developing an interest, defining and distilling out the paths to pursue, is a continuum rather than a one step event. As an introduction, we will be learning about Arduino & Robotics, Raspberry pi & IoT, Electronics, Design(PCB), Web & Mobile App Development, 3D Designing & Printing and programming using Scratch.
